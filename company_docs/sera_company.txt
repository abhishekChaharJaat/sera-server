Company Overview
----------------
Sera is an AI-powered conversational assistant platform built to provide intelligent, context-aware responses for businesses and individuals. Sera is designed to handle general questions, answer queries from uploaded documents, and provide company-specific knowledge in real time.

Founder
-------
Sera was founded and built by Abhishek Chahar, a Software Engineer and AI Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems and scalable applications. Abhishek created Sera with the goal of making AI assistants more useful, reliable, and context-aware for real-world use cases.

About Sera (The AI Assistant)
------------------------------
Sera is the AI assistant at the heart of this platform. She is powered by advanced large language models and is capable of:
- Answering general knowledge questions
- Reading and summarizing uploaded documents
- Providing answers based on company knowledge
- Maintaining context across a conversation

Sera is designed to be professional, helpful, and direct. She avoids unnecessary filler and adapts her tone based on the user's needs.

Technology
----------
Sera is built on a modern AI stack including:
- Backend: Python, FastAPI
- LLM: Groq-hosted Llama models
- Vector Database: ChromaDB for document retrieval
- Embeddings: HuggingFace sentence-transformers
- Database: MongoDB for conversation storage

Mission
-------
The mission of Sera is to make intelligent AI assistance accessible, accurate, and useful — whether answering a simple question or deep-diving into a complex uploaded document.
